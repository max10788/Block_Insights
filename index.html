How It Works

Simple by Design

I built this to be useful from minute one — no five-page onboarding, no sales call required.

01
Pick Your Chains & Wallets
Tell BlockInsights what you care about — which blockchains, which wallets to watch, how large a move needs to be before you hear about it.
02
It Watches. You Don't Have To.
BlockInsights indexes transactions in real time, classifies wallet behavior, spots OTC patterns, and flags hidden orders — all without you needing to stare at a screen.
03
You Get the Signal
Instant alerts on the dashboard, via webhook, or through the API. Take the signal into your own workflow — trade, research, or build on top of it.
